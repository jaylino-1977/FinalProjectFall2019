About J. Lino

John Lino Paris (J.Lino) began his lifestyle of healthy eating 5 years ago after being told by his physician that he was 1 point away from being full blown diabetic. John’s wife Jane suffered from high cholesterol and high blood pressure for years.

After 12 months of healthy cooking at home both have been able to reverse the medical conditions that once were overtaking them. John and Jane live in San Antonio, Texas with their two children.

Enjoy a library full of traditional and modern recipes that encompass healthy eating and promote a healthy lifestyle.
